The boundary

The bot can help the business draft, organize, remind, research, and report. It should not quietly get access to the owner's whole private digital life.

Mission

Useful automation needs a gate.

We protect the thin division between autonomous business bot devices and personal accounts devices. That means dedicated hardware, business-only profiles, client-owned AI accounts, limited permissions, and human review where the stakes are high.

Separate the machine

Run business automation from dedicated hardware instead of a personal daily-use laptop.

Separate the accounts

Use client-owned AI/API billing, business email, and approved service credentials.

Limit the permissions

Give the bot access to the workflow it needs, not every account the owner has.

Keep review where it matters

Use human approval for sensitive, financial, legal, medical, or reputation-risky actions.
